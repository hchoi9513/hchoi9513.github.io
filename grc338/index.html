Introduction

Heliophilia Eyewear

Heliophilia is a high-end sunglasses brand that captures the marriage of beauty and functionality in our classic but edgy designs. Heliophilia products elevate simplicity with unique shapes and creative usage of materials.

Target audience

Our company will target the young adult to middle-aged adult demographic, ages ranging from 19 to 60 years, and those of all genders. We expect our consumer base to have a fairly affluent lifestyle and/or values quality purchases.

Users of this site will be searching/shopping for a pair of sunglasses that firstly, have premium-quality UV resistance, but secondly, make a refreshing style statement while achieving their minimalist style prefences.
